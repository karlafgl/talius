    Vandalism Protection

    Protection from Costly Vandalism

    Talius Rollshutters provide protection against vandalism. Strong, safe and durable, they are a reliable barrier to protect your property against crimes of opportunity.

    Talius Rollershutters have undergone impact and strength testing and are designed to prevent vandalism and forced entry. They blend seamlessly with the building envelope to create a pleasing environment for customers, employees, and students by eliminating the locked appearance of other traditional security products.

    Rollshutters are perfect for

    • Commercial buildings
    • Residential buildings
    • School buildings
    • Modular buildings
    • Cottages & vacation homes

    If graffiti is a concern, we offer an Anti-Graffiti coating to protect your Rollshutter from graffiti damage.

    Protect your home or business from intrusion and business opportunities with the help of Talius’ vandalism protection products.

    Talius Anti-Graffiti Coating

    Talius Anti-Graffiti coating is a highly cross-linked polyurethane coating designed for maximum surface protection against crayons, markers, paint and chemicals. Making it an ideal choice for areas often targeted by vandalism.

    To preserve your image and save money on clean up costs, choose Talius Anti-Graffiti coating.
