    Energy Efficiency

    Increase the Efficiency of Your Building with Talius

    Talius manufactures innovative energy-saving products that help improve the quality of the indoor environment. Talius' energy-efficient products improve thermal comfort and reduce on demand energy use for heating and/or cooling.

    Habitat Screens

    Talius Habitat Screens are an excellent energy-efficient solution. They are installed on the outside of the window and block the sun's heat and glare before it heats up the indoor environment.

    Talius Habitat Screens are like sunglasses for our home. When in the down position, they block the sunlight without blocking your view and when not in use, they retract out of sight into an overhead panel box. Even more energy savings can be gained when our Habitat Screens are equipped with a sunlight sensor and/or integrated into building automation systems. Save significantly on cooling energy costs with Talius Habitat Screens, while reducing your carbon footprint.

    Rollshutters

    Talius Rollshutters are an effective solution to prevent solar heat gains in summer and reduce heat loss through windows in winter. Installed on the outside of your property or business, Talius Rollshutters are insulated to absorb heat and cold, and will save you money on energy costs. Rollshutters can be easily operated from the inside and are fully retractable. If you are searching for an effective energy efficient solution, Rollshutters are a wise choice, allowing you to experience optimal thermal comfort while reducing your environmental carbon footprint.

    Energy savings will vary depending on the location, direction of the building during peak energy demand and the location of the users of the roller shutters.

    Benefits of Talius Energy Efficiency Products

    Energy savings will vary depending on the climate zone you are located in, the size and positioning of the building, and the building envelope to name a few. Occupant positioning of the Rollshutter or Habitat Screen during peak energy demand plays an important role as well. Talius recommends integrating smart controls to ensure peak performance and to maximize energy savings.

    • Reduce heating and cooling energy costs
    • Block harmful UV rays
    • Optimize thermal comfort
    • Reduce the carbon footprint
