    Awnings

    Talius Awnings – Embrace the Solace of Outdoor Living

    You deserve the best, and Talius ensures that your awning will exceed your expectations of quality and style. Our awnings are suitable for both commercial and residential use where shade is a necessity. We proudly supply much of the Caribbean including Barbados, St. Lucia, Grenada, Dominica, Antigua, and both the US and British Virgin Islands. Talius awnings are fully customizable to suit all of your sun protection needs.

    Easy to Operate

    All Talius awnings are tested for ease of use. For added convenience, we also offer remote controls, wind sensors, and sun and wind sensors.

    Built To Last

    Talius is the largest awning manufacturer in North America using superior Swela® fabric. Swela® fabrics are coated with a Teflon® finish to repel dirt, grease, and oil to protect your fabric and lengthen the life of your product.

    Awnings protect against damages from the sun’s UV rays, which can fade the upholstery on furniture. Our commercial and residential solutions can also help you save money by limiting your energy consumption, thus reducing your carbon footprint.

    All Talius awnings feature a 5-year manufacturer’s limited warranty.

    Which Awning is Right for You?

    Santana

    Santana awnings are designed for excellent performance and contemporary style. Your affordable and practical choice for extending your outdoor living space and relaxing in comfort.

    Primasol

    Primasol semi-cassette awnings combine a unique design with heavy duty onstruction. When closed the curved front bar aligns with the aluminum awning hood to protect he fabric from dirt, birds, and all weather conditions.
